ABOUT US

Who We Are

Founded by trained Geospatial Engineers, ZeroPoint Geospatial was built on a simple observation: too many projects in Kenya stall because spatial information was vague, missing, or poorly documented.

Precision Data. Smarter Decisions.

We combine academic training with hands-on field experience to deliver accurate, decision-ready spatial data. Our workflows follow strict QA/QC protocols, and every deliverable is structured for immediate use by owners, consultants, and construction teams.

Land in Kenya is one of the most valuable assets in the country. We exist to map it right, one survey at a time — using modern GNSS/RTK equipment, structured capture methods, and clear communication throughout every project.

QUICK ANSWERS

Survey Quote FAQ

A few details help us price and plan the work properly. The upgraded quote form asks for those details upfront.

What information do I need for a survey quote?
Share the project location, land size, service type, urgency, access notes, and any existing title, deed plan, drawing, coordinates, or site sketch. More context helps us quote faster.
What deliverables can I request?
You can request PDF maps, CAD drawings, coordinate tables, survey reports, GIS layers, topographic data, or construction setting-out notes depending on the project.
Do you support projects outside Nairobi?
Yes. ZeroPoint Geospatial is based in Nairobi and can discuss survey and mapping work across Kenya depending on project scope, access, and timelines.
How much does a land survey cost in Kenya?
Pricing depends on land size, terrain, accessibility, and deliverable type. We provide transparent, itemized quotes within 2 hours of receiving project details. No hidden fees.
How do I verify land beacons before buying?
Request a boundary verification survey. We locate existing beacons, cross-reference with title deed coordinates, and provide a signed report confirming parcel alignment and encroachments.
