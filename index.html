FAQ

What information do I need for a quote?▼
Share project location, land size, service type, urgency, and any existing plans or coordinates.
What deliverables can I request?▼
PDF maps, CAD drawings, coordinate tables, survey reports, GIS layers, or topographic data.
Do you work outside Nairobi?▼
Yes. We can discuss survey work across Kenya depending on project scope and timelines.
How much does a survey cost?▼
Pricing depends on land size, terrain, and deliverable type. We provide transparent quotes within 2 hours.
How do I verify land beacons?▼
Request a boundary verification survey. We locate beacons and provide a signed report confirming parcel alignment.
